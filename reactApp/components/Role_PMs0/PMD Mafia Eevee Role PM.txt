You are Eevee one of the possible playable characters in Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Red & Blue Rescue Teams and Explorers of Sky.  You’re a normal type pokemon that can evolve into one of eight different possible pokemon and types depending upon the situation. In this game however your choice of eeveelutions will be Espeon or Umbreon as they played an important role in researching the Magnagates and enabling the pokemon from the Paradise to reach the Great Glacier and combat the Bittercold.

As an Eevee one of your abilities is Run Away, therefore you are the [B]Evolving 1x Commuter[/B] whilst in this form.  From day 3 or night 3 onwards you can evolve into either Espeon or Umbreon by submitting the action [B]Evolve[/B] to the hosts during the day or night phase, day for Espeon or night for Umbreon.  Evolution will be instantaneous and you can use your new abilities immediately.

In short, you are [b]Evolving 1x Commuter[/b] allied with the [b][color=green]Rescue Teams[/color][/b] and you win when all threats to [b][color=green]town[/color][/b] are eliminated.

[spoiler][IMG]https://s19.postimg.org/wc5l8k0sj/PMD_Espeon.png[/IMG][/spoiler]

As Espeon is a psychic type pokemon you can sense the presence of danger, you are therefore the [B]2x Sensor[/B].  Twice per game during the night you may submit the action [B]Sensor[/B] to be informed of how many mafia players were on the majority vote bandwagon during the previous day.

In short, you are [b]2x Sensor[/b] allied with the [b][color=green]Paradise[/color][/b] and you win when all threats to [b][color=green]town[/color][/b] are eliminated.

[spoiler][IMG]https://s19.postimg.org/ux40k07gz/PMD_Umbreon.png[/IMG][/spoiler]

As Umbreon is a nocturnal dark type pokemon you blend easily into the night allowing you follow others without being noticed, you are therefore the [B]2x Follower[/B].  Twice per game during the night you may submit the action [B]Follow (Player)[/B] to follow that player during the night and be informed of the actions they used.

In short, you are [b]2x Follower[/b] allied with the [b][color=green]Paradise[/color][/b] and you win when all threats to [b][color=green]town[/color][/b] are eliminated.
